Instructions

This is SMASH - the game that simulates a car race. Respond with one of the following maneuvers when a '?' is shown:

  • 1 - FLOOR IT
  • 2 - ACCELERATE (MODERATE)
  • 3 - BRAKE SLIGHT
  • 4 - JAM ON THE BRAKES
  • 5 - SHARP RIGHT
  • 6 - MODERATE RIGHT
  • 7 - SHARP LEFT
  • 8 - MODERATE LEFT

Track positions are as follows:

  • 1 - THE START LINE
  • 2 - MID STRAIGHT-AWAY
  • 3 - COMING UP ON A LEFT TURN
  • 4 - MID LEFT TURN
  • 5 - COMING UP ON A RIGHT TURN
  • 6 - MID-RIGHT TURN
  • 7 - THE FINISH LINE
